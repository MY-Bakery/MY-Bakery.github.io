ABOUT US

MY Bakery originated from the stone ages, where Yaren Sayilan and Merel van Iterson were holbewoners. The first cake they created was their famous and delicious, 'Pou taart'. Unfortunately the first Pou taart led to people having voedselvergiftiging. After lots of time, now in 2023 they have a lot more fans, customers and cakes.
